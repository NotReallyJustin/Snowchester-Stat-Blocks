# A Note on Mark Iron
Mark is (or at least, is very likely to be) the BBEG of a campaign that messes with Space and Time. He's not intended to be _good_ in combat and should be played like a support character.

The main gimmick of the boss fight is **Time Ravage** (not to be confused with the 9th level spell). Mark shatters Space and Time and brings back some of the other Vanguards (albeit, weaker versions of themselves) back from the dead. 

If you're curious, see the Stat Blocks for:
* Philip III (Fort Bill)
* Sebastian Scarsdale (Vel Calem)
* Benjamin Korolev (Snowchester)
* Dr. Nicholas Castino (Fenwick)
* Evan Ryder (Badlands)
* Mitchell Auberano (Takeshiam)

## Using Mark in your Campaigns
Mark's the most technologically advanced person in the campaign, just by nature of how IronVale is and because of the Chronolens. For _most_ campaigns (read: all non-futuristic/sci-fi campaigns), you will need to significantly reflavor him.
